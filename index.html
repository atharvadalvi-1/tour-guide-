Trip blueprint

One night, two days, zero chaos

Day 1: Arrival + sunset magic

  • Leave early in the morning and reach Panchmarhi by afternoon.
  • Check in, freshen up, and walk to the scenic viewpoints.
  • Enjoy snacks, music, a little football, and a relaxed dinner.

Day 2: Adventure + return

  • Wake up for sunrise views and a light breakfast.
  • Visit key spots like Bee Falls, Dhoopgarh, and local markets.
  • Pack up, click group photos, and head back with smiles.
